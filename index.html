Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the event. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us directly.

What time does the event start and end?

The cocktail event begins at 6:00 PM, followed by dinner at 7:30 PM. The evening will conclude around 11:00 PM. We recommend arriving by 5:45 PM for check-in. While the main wedding event begins at 11:00 AM.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is cocktail attire. For men, we suggest dress pants with a collared shirt or blazer. For women, cocktail dresses, dressy separates, or elegant pantsuits are appropriate and for the main wedding event, main colours of the day are white & purple.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, complimentary valet parking is provided for all guests. There's also a self-parking garage adjacent to the venue if you prefer. Both options are included with your RSVP.

Will food and beverages be provided?

Absolutely! The evening includes a full three-course dinner with vegetarian and dietary restriction options available. An open bar will be available throughout the event featuring wine, beer, and signature cocktails.

Can I bring a guest or plus-one?

Guest policies vary by invitation type. Please check your invitation or contact us directly to confirm if your RSVP includes a plus-one. Additional guests may be accommodated based on availability.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, The Heritage Hotel is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restroom facilities. Please let us know in advance if you have any specific accessibility needs so we can ensure your comfort.

What happens if I need to cancel my RSVP?

We understand plans can change. Please notify us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. You can update your RSVP through the form above or contact us directly. Cancellations help us accommodate others on our waiting list.

Will there be photography at the event?

Yes, we'll have a professional photographer capturing moments throughout the evening. Photos will be shared with all attendees after the event. If you prefer not to be photographed, please let us know upon arrival.
